Brandon George is a geospatial data scientist based in California. He has a master's degree in Geographic Information Science from California State University, Long Beach and a demonstrated history of expanding the horizon by setting goals and learning new skills in order to excel in the field. He is skilled in analytical writing, investigative research, web development, graphic design, content creation, spatial analysis, data analysis, data management, programming, remote sensing, and geographic information systems (GIS). He strives to make an impact by focusing on design that builds bridges between information and understanding. His interests range from exploring nature to making music.
